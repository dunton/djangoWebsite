Podcasts


I am an avid podcast listener and have curated some of my favorite ones below!

  • Exponent
    • Podcast focused on the business side of technology companies hosted by Ben Thompson and James Allworth. Ben Thompson also publishes daily pieces on Stratechery.
  • How to Start a Startup
    • Sam Altman, of Y Combinator fame, taught a course at Stanford on startups. Altman brought in celebrities from Silicon Valley in each class to give a lecture on their own area of expertise. Guest lecturers include Peter Thiel, Paul Graham, Keith Rabois, Reid Hoffman, Ben Horowitz, Marc Andreessen and more. This podcast is a compiliation of each of these lectures.
  • Internet History Podcast (Core Episodes Only)
    • A great podcast for quickly touching base on the history of the internet and the companies created because of it. I would recommend to only listen to the core episodes and not the interviews, though those are interesting as well.
